# MetaBrain Brain Expression

## Summary

MetaBrain is a large-scale meta-analysis of brain expression data across multiple brain regions and cohorts. The resource aggregates RNA-seq data from multiple independent studies to enable robust eQTL discovery across brain regions relevant to neurological and psychiatric disorders.

## Dataset Information

- **Cohorts**: Multiple cohorts (see [MetaBrain.nl](https://www.metabrain.nl/))
- **Brain regions**: Cerebellum, Cortex, Basal ganglia, Spinal cord, Hippocampus
- **Data type**: Bulk RNA-seq
- **Samples**: ~2,000+ samples per region (region-specific)

## Omics Data

### Gene expression matrices

Expression matrices (TMM-normalized counts) available from [MetaBrain.nl](https://www.metabrain.nl/).
